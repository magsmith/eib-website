About

Engineering Campus Tours leave from the Spurlock Museum at 2:00 p.m. every Monday through Friday during the school year. To attend a tour, simply go to the front desk of the Spurlock Museum and say you are there for an Engineering Campus Tour.

There is no need for prior registration for this event. EIB is the only university agency that offers such tours of the engineering campus.

Approximately 2,000 people attend Engineering Campus Tours every semester. Our tour guides are current engineering students who provide current information about our departments and research, as well as first-hand experiences from their time in Engineering at Illinois. After the tour is completed at 3:30 pm, either a dean or an academic advisor will offer a session where you may ask more questions. This session does not require prior registration, and will be the ending point of the Engineering Campus Tour.

Special tours may be arranged by emailing the Engineering Campus Tours Director at eib-tours@illinois.edu, or by calling (217) 244-3828.

The Campus Visitors Center welcomes prospective undergraduate students with programs that are held at 10:00 a.m. and 1:00 p.m. Monday through Friday (except some holidays). Each program includes a media presentation, admission and housing information, and a general campus tour.
