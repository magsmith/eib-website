Engineering Days

About

Welcome to Engineering Days for high school students! Engineering Days is one of the best ways for prospective engineering students to see what being an engineer is all about. The University of Illinois and Engineering Council would like to invite you to attend the next program!

As a high school student, you have probably begun to consider different majors of study and which school to attend after graduation. You will have many questions. Engineering Days can begin to help answer these questions even if you're merely thinking about a career in engineering.

During the course of the day, you will learn about the different engineering fields, visit cutting-edge labs, talk to current college students about life as an engineering student, and get a good look at one of the nation's top rated engineering universities. For more information on what a typical Engineering Day is made up of, please see the sample schedule below.
